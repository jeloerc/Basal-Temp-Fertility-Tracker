Getting Started

1 Make sure your server is running

The app connects to your existing Basal Temperature Tracker backend at http://your-server-ip:5000

2 Install Expo Go on your phone

Available for free on both iOS App Store and Google Play Store

3 Scan the QR code

Using your device's camera or the Expo Go app

4 Update server address

On first launch, enter your server's IP address when prompted

App Features

Advanced Visualization

Interactive charts with different colors for follicular and luteal phases. Mark period days with special indicators.

Cycle Analysis

Statistical analysis of your cycle patterns, including average cycle length and temperature shifts.

Fertility Tracking

Clear visual indicators for likely fertile days based on your temperature patterns.

Historical Data

Browse through past cycles to identify patterns and track changes over time.

Temperature Conversion

Easily switch between Fahrenheit and Celsius temperature units.

Detail On Demand

Tap any data point to see exact temperature readings and additional notes.
